The galaxy is in turmoil. Plasma cannonfire blossoms across the skies of 306 Soda as an age-old battle rages on. On one side, the Stylactic Republic. Champion to centuries of carefully written, stylesheet-heavy webpages, the Republic stands for peace, prosperity, and <link rel="stylesheet"> tags. Its opponent: the Confederacy of HTML-Only Systems, which has openly renounced the "causeless" temptresses known to the people as CSS and JavaScript. Sadly, more and more are swayed to the Confederacy's will each day. In desperation, the Republic has created many templates (such as the one you're looking at right now) in hopes of bringing its enemies back to the light. Will the plan succeed? The rest of the story is yet to be written...
