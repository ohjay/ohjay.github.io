CS 184: Computer Graphics and Imaging

Semester recap and reflections

Hello all, my name's Owen and I'll be your rambling internet persona for the day. A week ago, I finished CS 184 – UC Berkeley's upper-division graphics course – after a semester of general confusion and periodic project hell. I'm already missing it. Along with 188, CS 184 was one of the most interesting upper-division courses I've taken so far. Sure, it was math-y and physics-y and hard for me to follow, but at the end of the day it actually changed the way I see the world. More specifically, it's changed the way I see myself seeing. (Meta huh?)
