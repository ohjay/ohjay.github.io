Let's Talk About Bagels

I, Owen Jow, am a huge fan of bagels. Especially good bagels. I created this webpage as an outlet for me to evaluate and worship God's gift to bread products, while also facilitating my search for the greatest bagel of all time (GBOAT).

Using this site as a platform, I plan to document the best (and worst) bagels of the contemporary era. Over time, I even hope to create a comprehensive bagel record that can be referenced by enthusiasts all around the world.
